# v0.4.1 Multicolor Panel Outlines

## What changed
- Gradient color now wraps all four sides of major cards.
- Quest, diary, activity, progression, unlock, analytics, and player cards have distinct color mixes.
- Existing selectable themes still work.
- Mobile glow is reduced for readability and performance.

## Test
1. Open Command Center and inspect each analytics card.
2. Change Interface Theme in Settings.
3. Open Quests and Diaries.
4. Hover cards and verify the outline remains around the full panel.
5. Test on mobile or narrow browser width.
