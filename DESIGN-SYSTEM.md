# HCIM Progression Design System — v0.4.0

## Typography
- **Cinzel**: application title, route headings, important card headings.
- **Inter**: body copy, controls, navigation, forms.
- **Roboto Condensed**: percentages, counters, and compact statistics.

## Color semantics
- Gold: primary actions and current objectives.
- Blue: information, Wiki links, and travel.
- Green: completed, verified, and connected.
- Orange: recommendation and attention.
- Red: danger, destructive actions, and hardcore risk.
- Purple: elite diaries and rare progression.

## Themes
- Classic Gold
- Hardcore Ember
- Runite Blue
- Elite Purple

Theme selection is saved locally per browser.

## Component rules
- One base raised-panel treatment for cards.
- Primary buttons are gold; information links are blue; destructive actions are red.
- Progress bars animate but honor `prefers-reduced-motion`.
- Sidebar active state uses a bright left rail and restrained glow.
- Toast messages should be brief and disappear automatically.
