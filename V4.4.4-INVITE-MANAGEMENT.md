# v4.4.4 Invite Management

- Owner-only invite creation and revocation through authenticated Netlify Functions.
- Expiring invitations (24 hours, 3 days, or 7 days).
- Configurable limited uses, capped by remaining group slots.
- Invite preview before joining shows group name, capacity, expiry, and remaining uses.
- Legacy v4.4.0 invite migration remains supported.
- Invite documents remain inaccessible to browser Firestore clients.

## New Netlify Functions
- `group-invite-manage`
- `group-invite-preview`

No Firestore rule change is required from v4.4.0 because invites are already server-only.
